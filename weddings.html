Wedding FAQs

Thank you for considering me for your big day! Below you'll find some useful information about my wedding package. If you'd like to learn more or book me for your wedding, please email me or use the form below.

What Can We Expect From You As Our Photographer?

For starters, when you book with me, you actually get two photographers for your special day! My husband, Jordan, graduated with a degree in Visual Communications Technologies with a specialization in web, photo, and print. He is my second shooter for all weddings so none of your special moments are missed. We feel a huge responsibility when you choose us to capture the most important day of your life and we feel as though we are the safe-keepers of your most cherished memories. We aim to provide anything you need from us on your wedding day and our main goal is that you feel relaxed and comfortable so you can enjoy your day completely. Jordan and I will help you be yourself and fun in front of the camera for portraits and group shots, but we are always mindful to keep our presence low-key for the intimate parts of the day. If you're interested, let's meet up for coffee and we can chat some more!

Do you travel?

Yes! Although there may be a travel charge for expenses/accommodations, we love shooting in new places. Areas that do not require a travel charge: Cincinnati, Columbus, Dayton, Toledo, Lexington, and Northern Kentucky.

What are your prices?

2017 weddings cost $1500. This includes all day coverage from Chelsey and Jordan, 400-600 signature edited images, online gallery with full resolution photos, and print rights to all photographs. We strongly encourage also booking an engagement session with us to get comfrotable with us in front of our camera! If you book your wedding with us, you get $50 off of your engagement session.

How long will it take to get access to our online gallery?

4-6 weeks maximum.

How do I secure my wedding date with you?

After we get together to discuss details, you will recieve a contract to be signed digitally, as well as an invoice for a booking deposit. The signed contract, along with the deposit of 50% of the total price, will officially book Chelsey + Jordan for your wedding day!

What should we wear to our engagement shoot?

Be yourselves and have fun with your outfit! Consider wearing complimenting outfits as opposed to "matching" outfits so you can work off of a color pallet while each getting to show off your individual style while still looking great together.
